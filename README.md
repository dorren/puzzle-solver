## Introduction

This is a puzzle solver for the following. Given 4 10s,
use + - * / and lg() to solve.

```
  10   10   10   10 =  1
  10   10   10   10 =  2
  10   10   10   10 =  3
  10   10   10   10 =  4
  10   10   10   10 =  5
  10   10   10   10 =  6
  10   10   10   10 =  7
  10   10   10   10 =  8
  10   10   10   10 =  9
  10   10   10   10 = 10
```

## Run
```
ava install --global
npm install
npm test   # or   npm run test
```
